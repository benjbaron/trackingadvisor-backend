Privacy Policy

Thank you for participating in this study! The main goal of this study is to assess the correctness of the personal information that we have automatically extracted from places that you selected. We also want to use this study to understand your privacy expectations associated to the places and the personal information.

The information that we collect

To this end, we collect a certain number of personal information:

  • A unique session identifier that is created when you first visit this page.
  • Your current IP address.
  • The list of places that you have chosen associated to the unique identifier.
  • The information you have provided about the places and the personal information associated to the unique identifier.
Why we collect this information

We need this information to successfully conduct our study and understand the different aspects mentioned in the introduction.
We also collect this information to monitor and protect this website.
